Fragment: Sengen Taisha

Fujisan Hongū Sengen Taisha is the head shrine of approximately 1,300 Sengen shrines across Japan, dedicated to the deity Konohanasakuya-hime — goddess of flowers, and of the transient beauty of life. The shrine has historically governed access to Mt. Fuji's sacred grounds.

_Its satellite site at the summit, long unmanned, was reactivated in MF33 by a former Beast Hunter who filed no formal paperwork and listed his occupation as: monk._
