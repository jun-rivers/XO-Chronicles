The _ Teahouse


H/O2 looked at Art —
at the rain falling through where Art's hands should have been —
and then held out what it had been carrying.

An umbrella.

Black, long-handled, slightly formal.

"You left this," H/O2 said.
"It was about to rain."

Leon looked at the umbrella
for a long moment.

He had not held it in twelve years.

He took it.

"Thank you, H/O2,"
he said.


Leon and Art arrives at the teahouse,
where they meet Xixi, who has been caring for the house.
They learned about the Teahouse and Tea plantations.

Xixi gave hint about Art's mother.
