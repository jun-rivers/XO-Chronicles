Volume 1, Chronicle 2
The Secret

"To whomever finding my son, please,
I beg you, keep him save.
and raise him into a good man.
His name is Arthur, and I am sorry:
his lastname is a secret I must refrain from telling you.
Begins the voice in the Halo.
When Arthur shall come to me one day and 
tell me who his foster parents were
I'll repay you with more water and batteries than you'd need for a lifetime"
Promised, the Voice.
"And when he has grown into a strong body and
learned a critical mind, please pass him this Halo."
Art stares at the glowing torus, as the light spins slowly
as if it is loading its memory.
"Arthur. when you are listening to this, that means
you have survived, and fallen into good hands.
For that alone I am grateful. 
And I do not know how many years it would have taken
for you to become strong and sharp,
but anything less, 
and given what I am about to ask you to do,
would equal to asking you to die."
Cold sweat flows down Art's neck
as he listens to this voice for the first time
who is supposed to be his father,
talking about his death.
"Your mother is very special, arthur,
and that makes you also unusual.
Simply put, most who knew of your existence think you are dead,
and its better that way.
Except,"
Came silence.
"You have a destiny. 
A mission."
