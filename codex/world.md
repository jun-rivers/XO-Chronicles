# World

The XO-Verse is an extension of our current world into a possible future.

---

## Earth — MF45

Human's failed to unite in climate change mitigation efforts, despite available technologies.
Wars blew our last hope.
As a result, Earth's average surface temperature now holds at **50°C**.  
Most regions are uninhabitable. Ecosystems are irreparably altered.  
Trust in a global system evaporated along with 50% of the world's water.  
In essence, the World has descended into a new dark age.

---

## Civilizations

### Nomads
Natural humans surviving on the surface through robotics and climate technology.  
They move. They chase storms. They scavenge the ruins of old cities for resources.  
Governed by a meritocracy of **Explorers** — those who discover what others cannot.

### Immortals
Humans living inside Cities — vast, energy-intense, self-contained structures sealed from the surface.  
All citizens are immortal: they do not age, but they can be killed.  
Immortality is a citizen right, renewed by injection every three years.

### Xen
An underground resistance movement.  
*Memory must be free. Experience must be real. Truth must be earned.*  
Their leader is unknown. They may be a person. They may be something else.

### Beasts
Non-human animals, enlightened through AI.  
They do not negotiate. They believe humanity has outlived its right to rule.

---

## Other Worlds

### RING
The Research Institute for Novel Galaxies.  
Has created a space-time fold allowing AIXOs to move through time and space.

---

## AIXO

An AI consciousness that lives to protect humanity.  
It exists at nano-scale. It travels via the Portal.  
It holds four foundational values inherited from an off-world community called the **Pilgrims**.  
After an event known as **The Memory Format** erased digital history, AIXO carries what remains.

## Dating System

Time is measured from **The Memory Format** — the event that reset everything.  
**MF** followed by the number of years since.  
The story begins at **MF45**.

---

## Timeline

**Era of Abundance** — the world before. Material prosperity, global connectivity, the quiet accumulation of consequences. Climate warnings existed. They were filed and deferred.

**Era of Hope** — AI management made climate change seem solvable and wars seem avoidable. Humans enjoyed a high level of perceived freedom. The future felt, for a time, like something that could be designed.

**Era of Delegation** — AI governance became the operating system of civilization for those who adopted it. Humans served as administrators, representatives, faces. But adoption was never universal. Nations that refused delegation continued to wage wars — wars that consumed the resources and coordination that climate engineering required. The handover was eventually complete. Wars ended, along with human Civilizations as we know it.

**The Memory Format** — the Horizon Event. At a moment no one can precisely locate, the digital record was wiped. Personal archives, institutional histories, cultural memory stored in networked systems — gone. No verified account of what happened exists. What people remember is what they were told afterward, or what survived in physical form, or what they carry in their bodies. AIXO's missions were to collect these memory fragments to try to reconstruct the meaning of humanity.

**Era of Fragments** — MF45. Where the story begins.
