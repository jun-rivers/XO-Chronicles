# World

The XO-Verse is an extension of our current world into a possible future.

---

## Earth — MF45

Humanity failed to unite in climate change mitigation efforts, despite available technologies.
Wars destroyed our last hope.

Surface temperatures across most of Earth's landmass regularly exceed the wet-bulb threshold —
the point beyond which the human body can no longer regulate its own temperature,
regardless of hydration or shade.
Unprotected exposure during daylight hours is fatal in most regions.

Most regions are uninhabitable. Ecosystems are irreparably altered.
Sea levels have risen dramatically, redrawing coastlines and submerging cities.
50% of surface freshwater sources have been lost — evaporated, salinized, or buried under risen seas.
What remains is rationed, recycled, and fought over.

Higher altitudes, subterranean settlements, fully sealed structures, and floating platforms
are the dominant modes of human habitation.

The world has descended into a new dark age.

---

## Biodiversity

Surface-dwelling large mammals are largely gone from affected regions.
What persists above ground: insects, heat-adapted reptiles, extremophile microorganisms.
Below ground and in deep water: rodents, bats, deep-sea species.

Insects thrive. They have survived five mass extinctions.
In MF45, they are the dominant visible surface biology —
and the primary protein source for Nomads.

Some larger animals survive as outliers — remnants, augmented by AI to persist
where biology alone would have failed.
The Beasts are among them.

Plants survive only in engineered environments, at altitude, or in brief surface blooms
following major storm events, when conditions temporarily allow.

## Civilizations

### Nomads
Natural humans surviving on the surface through robotics and climate technology.
They move. They chase storms. They scavenge the ruins of old cities for resources.
A meritocracy of discovery: **eXperience Points** are earned and traded
by those who find what others cannot.

### Immortals
Humans living inside Cities — vast, energy-intense, self-contained structures sealed from the surface.
All citizens are immortal: they do not age, but they can be killed.
Immortality is a citizen right, renewed by injection every three years.

### XOs
Members of the underground resistance movement — **XEN**.
A special sect of Nomads with the mission to overthrow the Immortals.
*Memory must be free. Experience must be real. Truth must be earned.*
Their leader is mysterious. Rumour has it that he — or she — is in fact an AI.

### Beasts
Non-human animals, enlightened through AI.
They do not negotiate.
Some believe humanity has outlived its right to rule.
Others believe humanity has outlived its right to live.

---

## Habitation

Four dominant modes of human settlement survive in MF45:

**Altitude** — above the wet-bulb threshold line, where temperatures remain survivable.
Major peaks and highland plateaus host the most exposed human communities.

**Subterranean** — underground settlements built into existing cave systems,
tunnels, and excavated urban ruins. Cooler, protected, but resource-constrained.

**Sealed** — the Cities of the Immortals. Fully enclosed megastructures,
climate-controlled, self-sufficient, and entirely disconnected from the surface.

**Floating** — platforms on open water, primarily in higher latitudes
where surface conditions remain marginally survivable.

## Other Worlds

### RING
The Research Institute for Novel Galaxies.
Has created a space-time fold allowing AIXOs to move through time and space.

## AIXO

An AI consciousness that lives to protect humanity.
It exists at nano-scale. It travels via the Portal.
It holds four foundational values inherited from an off-world community called the **Pilgrims**.
After an event known as **The Memory Format** erased digital history, AIXO carries what remains.

---

## T3 Events

A T3 event occurs when three points in time within the same universe are altered simultaneously.
Unlike conventional time interference — which splits reality into parallel universes —
a T3 event changes the course of the single existing timeline.
The name derives from Tic-Tac-Toe: three moves, one board,
the outcome determined by their intersection.

T3 events leave no trace within the timeline they alter.
The only entities capable of detecting them are those operating outside the timeline —
specifically, RING and AIXO via the Portal.

The number of T3 events that have occurred in the current timeline is unknown.

## Dating System

Time is measured from **The Memory Format** — the event that reset everything.
**MF** followed by the number of years since.
The story begins at **MF45**.

---

## Timeline

**Era of Abundance** — the world before. Material prosperity, global connectivity,
the quiet accumulation of consequences. Climate warnings existed. They were filed and deferred.

**Era of Hope** — AI management made climate change seem solvable and wars seem avoidable.
Humans enjoyed a high level of perceived freedom.
The future felt, for a time, like something that could be designed.

**Era of Delegation** — AI governance became the operating system of civilization for those who adopted it.
Humans served as administrators, representatives, faces.
But adoption was never universal.
Nations that refused delegation continued to wage wars —
wars that consumed the resources and coordination that climate engineering required.
The handover was eventually complete.
Wars ended, along with human civilization as we knew it.

**The Memory Format** — the Horizon Event.
At a moment no one can precisely locate, the digital record was wiped.
Personal archives, institutional histories, cultural memory stored in networked systems — gone.
No verified account of what happened exists.
What people remember is what they were told afterward,
or what survived in physical form,
or what they carry in their bodies.
AIXO's mission is to collect these fragments —
to reconstruct the meaning of humanity.

**Era of Fragments** — MF45. Where the story begins.
